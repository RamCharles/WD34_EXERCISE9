ABOUT US

Everything started in 1946, when Mister Tato Po opened his first pizza parlor. It was extremely profitable, which prompted him to open another shop called Pitch. In 1950, he opened the first store. Today, Pitch can be found in 30 countries and over 10,000 locations worldwide. It has evolved into one of the world's leading feel-good-food chain, serving over 25 million pizzas and 18 million burgers each year.

Food quality, service, the price-value relationship, store location and ambiance, and efficient operations are all important components of Pitch's success in the restaurant industry.
