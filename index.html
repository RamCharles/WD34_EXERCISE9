ABOUT US

Everything started in 1946, when Tato Po opened his first pizza and burger shop. It was extremely profitable, which prompted him to open another shop called Pitch. In 1950, he opened the first store. Today, Pitch can be found in 30 countries and over 10,000 locations worldwide. It has evolved into one of the world's leading feel-good-food chain, serving over 5 million burgers and 9 million pizzas each year.
